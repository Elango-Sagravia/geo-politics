It includes an invitation to participate in a <a href="https://link.geopoliticalsummary.com/gs-14-01-2025-f1-8-membership-action-plan">Membership Action Plan</a> (MAP). Each candidate gets a tailored plan that requires countries to implement political, legal, military, defense, and security reforms. Countries meeting NATO''s criteria may begin accession talks. After negotiations, the country signs the Accession Protocol, which every NATO member state must ratify. The final step is depositing the instruments of accession with the U.S. government, the treaty''s depositary.
